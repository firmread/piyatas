Aîmée [Am-May] earned Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Design from Bangkok, Thailand. She came to New York to pursue Master’s Degree in Interactive Media at Pratt Institute, Brooklyn, NY. She graduated with GPA 3.9 (First Honors with Distinction.)

Aîmée has deep interests in visual and interaction design. Her areas of expertise are UI and UX for both web and mobile application design. She has been educated about art and design since 2004 and experienced in working with design agencies, R&D labs, and startup companies.
